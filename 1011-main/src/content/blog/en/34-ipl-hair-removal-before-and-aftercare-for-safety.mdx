## Why aftercare is part of “IPL is safe”

Most safety problems come from treating inflamed or sensitized skin, and from adding irritation after treatment (sun, heat, harsh skincare). A simple care protocol reduces those risks.

## Before your IPL session

### Shave, don’t wax
Shaving keeps hair pigment in the follicle while removing surface hair. Waxing or tweezing removes the root, which can reduce effectiveness and increase irritation.

### Avoid sun exposure
If the user has tanned recently, delay IPL. Tanned skin raises pigment and heat absorption risk.

### Keep skincare gentle
Avoid strong irritants on the treatment area leading up to sessions. If the user is prone to sensitivity, simplify the routine.

## After your IPL session

### Cool down and moisturize
Gentle cooling and barrier-supporting moisturizer can reduce redness.

### Avoid heat and friction
Avoid saunas, hot showers, and tight clothing immediately after treatment on sensitive areas.

### Prioritize sun protection
UV exposure after IPL can worsen redness and pigment changes.
